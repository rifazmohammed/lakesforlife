Environment and Service to all

Starting with lake restoration, we move ahead to the larger issues of environment, Lakesforall encompassing all critical issues affecting our existence from reckless mining and environmental degradation in Bellary to Global Warming. The subjects would be dealt from both academic and field level points of views, with emphasis on learning based out of experience and established facts with no hearsay or conjectures. Our quality of life is directly proportional to the unpolluted natural surroundings in which we live. We owe our life to our environment and lets all redeem our pledge to give it the first priority in our lives.

 Social Service is the social dimension to environmental preservation, to be of service to humanity and express your creativity, professionalism and commitment through the speechless, expressionless and the untouched to help create a social order based on equity, in both sharing and collaboration of resources. I have done my own share of social service and would continue to use all my resources, opportunities and might to do the same in future as well. Thanks to Rotary, Goa for providing a chance to unravel my social service heart once again. In fondest memory of the time I spent delivering a lecture a Madgaon on 2nd Feb 2013 on the topic so close to my heart, Reaching Out to the Unreached, I initiate this critical component of this website.
